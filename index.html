About Me

My name is Gretchen and I have been teaching and tutoring for the last 10 years! I graduated with a degree in Cell and Developmental Biology at the University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB) in 2017. After graduation, I moved to Santiago, Chile where I worked as a translator and developed advanced Spanish language skills. I am currently living in Seattle, Washington, where I offer both in-person and virtual Science and Spanish classes to children, college students, and adults. In my free time, I love to travel, ski, play intramural sports, garden, and cook!

At UCSB I taught Biology to undergraduates in both a class setting and one-on-one. I also worked as a substitute teacher for the Davis Joint Unified School District, so I have experience teaching/tutoring elementary school students up to high school (K-12). Over the past years, I have taught a variety of science classes including AP Biology, college-level Biology, Chemistry, Genetics, and Biotechnology.

During my time in Chile, I was an English language teacher where I worked with business professionals to improve language proficiency. Upon my return to the States, I started a job at a tutoring company and taught Spanish course material to high school students. I have experience teaching Spanish to both children and adults, so if you’re an adult looking to improve your conversational Spanish, don’t hesitate to sign up! I can help with anything from improving Spanish fluency to preparing for exams. It’s all up to you or your child’s goals!

Please check out the different tutoring options I offer and send me a message with any questions you may have. I am also happy to chat over the phone to see if we or your child will be a good fit for my classes. Best of luck studying!

Spanish Lessons
